Rules

  • Kilmotres earned by cycling are divided by 3.
  • Every week the runners of the winning club enter a raffle to win a "NAME OF SPORTSWACH" with Nordic inside.
  • Every kilometre counts! Points are based on each divisions total size, so the smallest contribution counts towards your clubs total!

Information about the leaderboard

The leaderboard uses Strava's own API to collect the participating clubs activity.
No personal data is stored or processed.
The weeks end each sunday at 23:59 GMT+1.
